# RoutineTabs — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-11_

RoutineTabs is designed to be private by default. It does **not** collect, transmit,
sell, or share any personal data.

## What the extension stores
- **Your routines** — the names, URLs, schedules (days/time), and enabled state you create.
- **Your settings** — language (English/Korean) and catch-up preferences.

All of this is stored **locally on your device** using the browser's `chrome.storage.local`
API. It never leaves your computer and is never sent to the developer or any third party.

## What the extension does NOT do
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
- No remote servers, no accounts, no sign-in.
- No reading of your browsing history or the content of web pages. The extension does
  not request the `tabs` permission or any host permissions.

## Permissions and why they are needed
- **`alarms`** — to trigger your routines at the day/time you scheduled.
- **`storage`** — to save your routines and settings locally on your device.

## Network activity
The only outbound navigation the extension performs is:
1. Opening the tabs (URLs) **you** configured in your routines.
2. Opening the GitHub Sponsors page **only if you click** the optional "Sponsor" button.

No data about you is transmitted in either case.

## Data deletion
Removing the extension, or clearing its storage, permanently deletes all stored
routines and settings. There is no server-side copy.
